The Indian Premier League (IPL) 2024 Player Auction will feature 333 cricketers who are scheduled to go under the gavel in Dubai at the Coca-Cola Arena on December 19th, 2023. Out of 333 players, 214 are Indians and 119 are overseas players of which 2 players are from associate nations. The total capped players are 116, uncapped players are 215 and 2 from associate nations. A maximum of 77 slots are now available with up to 30 being slotted for overseas players. The Auction will start at 1:00 PM IST
